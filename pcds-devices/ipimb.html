IPIMB¶

class pcdsdevices.epics.IPM(prefix, *, data='', ioc='', name=None, parent=None, read_attrs=None, **kwargs)¶

Standard intensity position monitor. Consists of two stages, one for the diode and one for the target. This creates a scalar readback that is also available over EPICS.
